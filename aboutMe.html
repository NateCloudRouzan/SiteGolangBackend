Student Seal is a driveway restoration company founded and in 2016 by University of Washington student Nate Cloud-Rouzan and his friends. We provide service in the greater Seattle of western Washington. Visit our facebook Page to see some of our work as well as customer reviews!

Our mission is to promote and support youth entrepenuership as well as dignity in manual labor.
